Retrospective

1895

Creation of the Woodworking and Carpenter's Shop in Billund (Denmark).

1916

Ole Kirk Christiansen purchases the Woodworking Shop in Billund.

1934

The company is named LEGO, from the Danish phrase leg godt ("play well"). "LEGO" also means "I assemble" in Latin.

1947

Ole Kirk purchases the first plastic moulding machine in Denmark, and the company begins manufacturing plastic toys.

1949

LEGO begins producing similar plastic bricks, calling "Automatic Binding Bricks".

1969

The Duplo system, using larger bricks and targeting younger children (but also compatible with existing LEGO bricks) is released.

1986

LEGO Technic robots controlled by computers are placed in schools.

1996

LEGO.COM is launched.

1997

LEGO launches its first PC game, LEGO Island.

1999

LEGO produces the first licensed and movie theme with Lucasfilm, LEGO Star Wars.

2000

LEGO Studios is introduced.

2002

Company slogan changes from "Just Imagine..." to "Play On".

2014

Release of The LEGO Movie.
